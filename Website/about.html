Project Scope

Data used in this project

Scope and Market

Our recommendation system identifies the top 10 stocks in each of the 11 Nasdaq sectors based on market capitalization.

Stock Data Source

We source stock data from the Nasdaq API, covering a 33 months period from 1 January 2022 to 31 October 2024.

Other Data Sources

We source news articles from EventRegistry API, and World News API, and Youtube transcripts from Google API.

User Preferences & Personalization

Our system tailors stock recommendations based on user preferences gathered from the survey on the website. Users can adjust their sector priorities and stock attribute preferences to receive more relevant recommendations.

Stock Ranking Criteria

Stocks are ranked based on multiple factors, including price consistency, trading volume, momentum, and volatility. Our algorithm ensures a balanced selection that aligns with market trends and user preferences.

Sentiment Analysis Methodology

We use the NLP technique VADER to analyze sentiment in financial news and YouTube transcripts, allowing us to incorporate market sentiment into our recommendation system.

Cut off date

Due to our limited budget, our cut off date of the data means that our recommendations and evaluations are for 30 Jun 2024

Performance Metrics

We measure success through backtesting and Sharpe ratio comparisons. Each recommendation is evaluated based on historical accuracy.

Future Plans & Enhancements

Future developments may include collaborative filtering, expanded data sources, and improved user customization options. We aim to refine our model continuously for better accuracy and usability.
